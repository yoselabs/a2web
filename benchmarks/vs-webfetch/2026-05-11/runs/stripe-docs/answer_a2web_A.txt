## What is a Stripe PaymentIntent?

A **PaymentIntent** is a Stripe API object that tracks a payment from creation through checkout and handles the complete payment lifecycle. Each PaymentIntent typically correlates with a single shopping cart or customer session in your application.

## Key Characteristics

A PaymentIntent encapsulates:
- The amount to collect
- The desired currency
- Supported payment methods
- The customer's payment progress and status changes

## Purpose in the Payment Lifecycle

The PaymentIntent serves several critical functions:

1. **Complex Payment Flow Management** — It handles complex payment flows with status changes throughout the PaymentIntent's lifecycle, including triggering additional authentication steps when required.

2. **Automatic Authentication Handling** — The API automatically manages Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) and similar regulatory requirements.

3. **Prevents Payment Issues** — It eliminates common problems:
   - No double charges
   - No idempotency key issues

4. **Regulatory Compliance** — Prepares you for expansion to other countries and supports new regulations and regional payment methods (used alongside Setup Intents and Payment Methods APIs).

5. **Purchase Funnel Tracking** — Best practice is to create a PaymentIntent as soon as you know the amount (when checkout begins) to track your purchase funnel.

6. **State Tracking** — The object state helps track any failed payment attempts for a given cart or session, allowing you to reuse the same PaymentIntent if checkout is interrupted and resumes later.

In essence, the PaymentIntent is the orchestrator of the entire payment process, managing complexity and compliance while preventing common errors.
