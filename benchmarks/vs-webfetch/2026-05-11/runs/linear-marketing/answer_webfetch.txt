# Linear Product Overview

**Product:** Linear is a product development platform—an issue tracking and project management system designed for software teams.

**Primary Value Proposition:** The homepage headline states: "The product development system for teams and agents," emphasizing that Linear is "Purpose-built for planning and building products. Designed for the AI era."

The core value proposition centers on three themes:
- **Built for purpose** through practices of world-class product teams
- **Powered by AI agents** for workflows shared by humans and AI
- **Designed for speed** to reduce noise and help teams ship with high velocity

Linear positions itself as a modern alternative to traditional issue tracking, integrating AI agent capabilities throughout the product development workflow—from intake and planning through building, reviewing, and monitoring work.
