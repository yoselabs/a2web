This article demonstrates how to control an inverted pendulum in MATLAB Simulink using PID controllers without requiring mathematical formulas. The author uses the Multibody library to build the physical model and Simulink's automatic tuning feature to configure two PID controllers — one for pendulum stabilization and another for cart positioning. The complete system includes noise simulation, a Kalman filter, and successfully maintains the pendulum in an upright position while handling external disturbances.
