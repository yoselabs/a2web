This article is about implementing and tuning PID controllers in MATLAB Simulink for an inverted pendulum system. It demonstrates how to build a physics-based model of an inverted pendulum (with a cart) using Simulink's Multibody library and then automatically configure PID controllers to stabilize both the pendulum angle and cart position without requiring manual mathematical calculations. The article shows that modern simulation tools allow engineers to solve complex control problems through a graphical interface with automated tuning features, eliminating the need for traditional manual physics and control theory calculations.
