This article demonstrates how to design and tune a PID controller for an inverted pendulum system using MATLAB Simulink's Multibody library and automatic tuning tools. The author shows that complex control problems can be solved without manual calculations or deep physics knowledge—Simulink's automation handles the linearization and PID coefficient optimization. The complete model includes swing-up logic, a Kalman filter for noise reduction, and dual control loops for pendulum angle and cart position, with results shown through simulation and animation.
