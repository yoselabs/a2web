The article discusses using Simulink and Multibody to configure a PID controller for managing an inverted pendulum without requiring technical knowledge. It describes setting up a model with parameters for the pendulum and cart, adding noise, and incorporating a Kalman filter for robustness. The model includes two PID controllers for stabilizing the pendulum's angle and cart position, and a Kalman filter for smoothing disturbances. The article highlights the simplicity and effectiveness of using Simulink for this task, emphasizing the ease of tuning and updating the controller without manual calculations.
