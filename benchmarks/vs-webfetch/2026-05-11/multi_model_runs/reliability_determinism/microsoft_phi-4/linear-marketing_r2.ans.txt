The company sells a product development system designed for modern teams, particularly those incorporating AI workflows. The primary value proposition is that Linear sets a new standard for planning and building products, making product operations self-driving by turning conversations and customer feedback into actionable issues. It facilitates defining product direction through strategic roadmaps and PRDs, moving work forward across teams and agents, and reviewing PRs and agent outputs within its platform. Linear also provides project updates, analytics, and dashboards to help understand progress at scale.
