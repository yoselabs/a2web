The article discusses using Simulink and Multibody to configure a PID controller for managing an inverted pendulum without requiring technical knowledge. It describes setting up a model with parameters like mass and friction coefficients, and tuning the PID controller automatically. The model includes a Kalman filter to smooth disturbances and a Kalman filter for state access. The article highlights the simplicity and robustness of the model, which uses Simulink's capabilities to simulate and animate the pendulum's stabilization and movement.
