This article describes using Simulink and its Multibody library to model and control an inverted pendulum system without manual calculations. It details the process of automatically tuning PID controllers, stating that "Современные средства настройки коэффициентов PID регулятора позволяют решать подобные задачи нажатием пары кнопок." The author concludes that the resulting model is stable and was developed without using a single formula.
