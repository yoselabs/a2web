This article describes how to use Simulink and its Multibody library to model and control an inverted pendulum system without using any manual calculations or formulas. The author details assembling the plant model and then using Simulink's "Tune" feature to automatically configure PID controllers for both the pendulum's angle and the cart's position. The final model, which includes a swing-up mechanism and a disturbance, is shown to be stable and effective.
