The article discusses the implementation of a PID controller for stabilizing an inverted pendulum using Simulink. It highlights the ease of setting up such a system without needing deep mathematical or physical knowledge, thanks to modern tools that automate the tuning process. The article walks through the steps of modeling the pendulum and cart, setting up the PID controllers, and adding features like Kalman filters for noise reduction. The final model demonstrates a stable system that can handle disturbances.
