The company sells a product development system designed for teams and agents, focusing on AI workflows. The primary headline/value proposition is: "A new species of product tool" that sets a standard for planning and building products with AI at its core.

Key features include:
- Making product operations self-driving by turning feedback into actionable issues.
- Defining product direction through strategic roadmaps and PRDs.
- Moving work forward with AI agents assisting teams.
- Reviewing PRs and agent output within the platform.
- Understanding progress at scale with analytics and dashboards.
