Rust was originally designed by Graydon Hoare, who "created Rust in 2006" while working at Mozilla. The first public release (Rust 0.1) appeared on January 20, 2012.

Rust is known for its memory-safety mechanism called the "borrow checker", which prevents memory safety errors by tracking the "object lifetime of references at compile time" without conventional garbage collection.
