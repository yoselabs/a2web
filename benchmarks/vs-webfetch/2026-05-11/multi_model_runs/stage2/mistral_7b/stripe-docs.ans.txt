 A Stripe PaymentIntent is an API used for handling complex payment flows, tracking transactions from creation through checkout, and triggering additional authentication steps when required. It encapsulates details about the transaction such as supported payment methods, amount to collect, and desired currency. The purpose of a PaymentIntent in the payment lifecycle is to create and confirm a PaymentIntent on the server, pass its client secret to the client side for secure completion of the payment process, and monitor webhooks to detect successful or failed payments.
