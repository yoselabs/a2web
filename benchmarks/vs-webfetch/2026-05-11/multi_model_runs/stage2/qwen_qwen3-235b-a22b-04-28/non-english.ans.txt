This article describes implementing a PID controller in Simulink for an inverted pendulum system using MATLAB's Multibody library, enabling model creation "without knowledge of physics or mathematics" via prebuilt blocks. It highlights automatic PID tuning through "automatic linearization and tuning" and adding a Kalman filter to handle sensor noise, achieving stability with minimal manual calculations. The approach emphasizes simplicity, robustness, and handling disturbances like external impulses.
