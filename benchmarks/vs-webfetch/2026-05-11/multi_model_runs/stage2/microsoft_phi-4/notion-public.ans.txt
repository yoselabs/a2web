A Notion database is a collection of pages within Notion, where each item is its own page. This allows for detailed customization and organization of data, such as adding properties and views to contextualize and manage information. Unlike regular pages, databases can link to other data sources, enabling comprehensive data management within a single workspace. They are unique in their ability to visualize data in various formats like tables, lists, boards, and calendars, and can be locked to prevent structural changes by others. This feature distinguishes databases from regular pages by allowing users to manage data more dynamically and collaboratively.
