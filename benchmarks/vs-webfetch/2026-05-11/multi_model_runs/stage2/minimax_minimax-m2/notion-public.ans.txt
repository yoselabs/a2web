Based on the content, a Notion database is a collection of pages that helps manage and organize multiple items in one place. The key difference from a regular Notion page is that "Every item is its own page" within a database, allowing you to add properties like dates, status, and links to contextualize each entry. Unlike a single page, databases let you "visualize your data in different ways" using tables, calendars, boards, galleries, or lists, and you can view multiple data sources in one database.
